    About

    I first had the idea for this project while wandering around Pismo Beach trying to find a place to watch the Cal–SMU game with fellow Bears. This is meant to be a crowdsourced list of Cal Bars far and wide so you can always watch games with friends anywhere in Bear Territory.

    Please contact me with any questions, and Go Bears!
