    About

    I first had the idea for this project when I was wandering around Pismo Beach trying to find a bar to watch the Cal–SMU game and realized there was no real resource. This is meant to be a crowdsourced list of Cal Bars far and wide so you can always cheer on the Golden Bears with friends wherever you are. Please contact me with any questions, and Go Bears!
